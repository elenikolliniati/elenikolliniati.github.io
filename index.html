About me

Having concluded my studies as a chemist in the university of Athens, I am currently completing my Msc regarding hydrogen production from water. Some years ago, I started programming and I enrolled in several courses, realizing this was my passion. In order to pursue a career in data, I attended Big Blue's data science bootcamp. This program helped me understand in depth the concepts of Machine Learning and also handle various real-life projects. My hobbies include playing bridge, a team sport that keeps me focused under pressure and refines my problem solving skills and my logical way of thinking. I believe that my enthusiasm, my agile way of handling tasks, as well as my diversified knowledge makes me a valuable asset in companies that want to thrive.
